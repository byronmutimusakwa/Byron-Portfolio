About

Hi, I'm Byron Mutimusakwa. I'm a character illustrator and visual development artist. I enjoy creating compelling character designs, rich visual narratives, and bringing stories to life through art. When I'm not working on professional projects, I'm usually sketching characters or painting digitally. All my art is hand-drawn, even in a world full of AI-generated content.
